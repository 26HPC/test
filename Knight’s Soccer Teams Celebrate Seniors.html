Knight’s Soccer Teams Celebrate Seniors

Lucas Bower • 20 Oct 2022

  The Neumann Knights soccer boys and girls teams both tied on senior night. The Neumann girls soccer team tied with Troy High School at UPMC field Wednesday night with a final score 0-0. The girls also celebrated their seniors: Brianna Lisi, Jaden Nixon, and Lily Ried. 

The seniors of both teams were celebrated in between the two games with each senior being escorted by their parents and given a signed soccer ball from the team. Following the girl’s game, the boys team tied against the Montoursville Warriors with a score of 0-0. The boys team celebrated their seniors: Diego Cuevas, Andrew Mitchell, Mark Roman, Corey Agnew, Kane Wright, Angelo Fernandez, and John Brann.
