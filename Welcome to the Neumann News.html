Our goal here at the Newspaper Club will be to inform SJNRA families and students of the school on a variety of different topics including: news, events, sports, opinion, entertainment, and outside current events through the varying perspectives of Neumann students. 

This year, we will deliver the news through both print and digital newspapers, while maintaining various Neumann News social media accounts.
