Bishop visits Neumann

📷 Malissa Heim – Admissions & Marketing Director

Author • Oct 27 2022

Bishop Bambara of the Diocese of Scranton came to visit St. John Neumann Regional Academy on Wednesday, October 26th. The Bishop celebrated mass at St. Boniface Church with Fr. Glenn McCreary and Fr. Sean Carpenter. Following mass, the bishop visited with the students and faculty of both the elementary and high school campuses along with Kristen Donohue, Diocesan Secretary of Catholic Education and Superintendent of Catholic Schools.
